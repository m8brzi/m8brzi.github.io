Spanning-Tree Protocol (STP)

STP Fundamentals

Introduction to the basics of Spanning-Tree Protocol.

STP prevents loops in bridged or switched networks by dynamically disabling links between bridges or switches.

Key concepts:

  • Bridge Protocol Data Units (BPDUs)
  • Root Bridge Election
  • Port Roles (Root, Designated, Non-Designated)
  • STP States (Blocking, Listening, Learning, Forwarding)

For more information, you can refer to the STP Fundamentals documentation.
